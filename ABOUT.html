ABOUT US

EKKVASTRA is an independent streetwear design studio engineering premium, raw, oversized garments crafted for modern subcultures.

OUR ORIGIN

ESTABLISHED IN BENGALURU, 2024

EKKVASTRA was born out of a frustration with flimsy, fast-fashion basics. We wanted structured garments that hold their shape. Heavyweight fabrics that feel substantial. Fits that drape perfectly, with sizing optimized for drop-shoulder aesthetics.

Every piece we create is designed from scratch in our local studio, built from custom heavyweight cotton blends, pre-shrunk, and custom-dyed. We represent a commitment to slow, deliberate drops over mass production.

CORE BRAND VALUES

The rules we live by when designing streetwear culture.

QUALITY FIRST

We source only high-grade, long-staple cotton fibers. Our hoodies use 380-400 GSM fleece, and our tees use 240 GSM combed cotton. Built to endure.

UNISEX CUTS

Designed with versatile, drop-shoulder silhouettes. Relaxed chests, boxy crops, and broad armholes engineered to look spectacular on all body types.

CULTURE DRIVEN

Every print, seam detail, and graphic tells a story. Inspired by hip-hop, skate culture, and raw concrete cityscapes.
